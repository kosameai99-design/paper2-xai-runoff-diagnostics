# Data access

The release contains derived metrics and attribution summaries, not raw discharge observations or third-party geospatial products.

- MERV-Jp: obtain the runoff-prediction dataset from its cited Zenodo/HRL record.
- GRDC: obtain station discharge and watershed boundaries directly from GRDC under its data policy.
- ERA5-Land: obtain variables from the Copernicus Climate Data Store, DOI 10.24381/cds.e2161bac.
- Soil and terrain products: obtain them from the providers cited in the manuscript and SI.

The public archive does not include QObs time series, GRDC station mappings, raw coordinates, basin polygons, model training pickles, or third-party rasters.
